About Our Hotel

The Chipmunk's Castle is an all suite hotel nestled on 6 acres along the pristine Goan coastline. The castle, cradled by the majestic western ghat range along the confluence of the famous river, offers amazing view of the sea. With a mile-long exclusive beachfront, it is truly one of the most exquisite beach hotels in Goa. ... Amidst grand Portuguese style architecture, meticulously landscaped gardens, the signature spa, enjoy the luxury of oceanfront privacy and seclusion in true Indian style. Upon arrival, the resort welcomes you into the grand lobby with rustic chandeliers, antique long-handled ceiling fans and a grand staircase overlooking a hand-painting. The resort offers 100 Deluxe suites, 20 Luxury suites, 30 Executive suites. Other facilities include unique fine dining restaurants, water sports, Swimming Pool. The hotel is also equipped with seven banquet halls/meeting rooms, ideal for all types of corporate events, conference facility, meeting facility, weddings ceremonies and wedding receptions.
